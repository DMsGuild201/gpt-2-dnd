50ft, burrow 20ft, fly 90ft

26

Spellcasting

The One Below All is a 16th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save 20, 12 to hit with spell attacks). The One Below All has following cleric spells prepared:

Cantrips (at will): guidance, mending, resistance, thaumaturgy

1st level (4 slots): bane, guiding bolt, shield

2nd level (3 slots): hold person, spiritual weapon

3rd level (3 slots): pass without trace, spirit guardians

Legendary Resistance (3/Day)

If the One Below All fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

Psychic Defense

While the One Below All is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, its AC includes its Wisdom modifier.

Actions

Multiattack

The One Below All makes one attack with its shortsword. Or three attacks with either sword or two attacks with either sword or shield.

Shortsword

Melee Weapon Attack 17 to hit, reach 11 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d6 + 10) piercing damage.

Shield Bash

Melee Weapon Attack 17 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 17 (2d8 + 10) bludgeoning damage.

Legendary Actions

The The One Above All can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The The One Above All regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Captivating Presence (Costs 1 Action)

Each creature of the One Below All's choice that is within 120 feet of the One Below All and aware of it must succeed on a 20 Wisdom saving throw or become charmed by the One Below All 1 minute. While charmed in this way, a creature's speed is 0. If the charmed creature takes damage, it can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success. A creature whose saving throw is successful against the effect is immune to the Captivating Presence of all One Below All for 1 hour.

Ink Cloud (Costs 3 Actions)

A 20-foot-radius cloud of ink extends all around the One Below All when it ends its turn. The area is heavily obscured for 1 minute, although a significant current can disperse the ink. After releasing the ink, the One Below All can use the Dash action as a bonus action.
